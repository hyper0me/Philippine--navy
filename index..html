Philippine Navy Ships

The Philippine Navy is the naval warfare branch of the Armed Forces of the Philippines. It protects our nation's maritime interests and supports security missions through a fleet of versatile, mission-ready ships.

BRP Jose Rizal

A modern guided-missile frigate, vital for patrolling and engaging maritime threats.

BRP Davao del Sur

A strategic landing dock used for amphibious missions, disaster relief, and troop deployment.

BRP Gregorio del Pilar

An offshore patrol vessel for securing Philippine waters and supporting naval operations.
