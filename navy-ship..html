Philippine Navy Ships

The Philippine Navy is the naval warfare branch of the Armed Forces of the Philippines. It protects our maritime borders and conducts missions using modern and multi-role ships.

BRP Jose Rizal

A modern guided-missile frigate designed for surveillance, surface and anti-air warfare.

BRP Davao del Sur

A landing dock ship equipped for transport, amphibious, and humanitarian missions.

BRP Gregorio del Pilar

Originally a US Coast Guard ship, now repurposed as a patrol vessel for maritime security.
