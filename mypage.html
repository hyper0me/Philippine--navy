Philippine Navy Ships

The Philippine Navy is the naval warfare service branch of the Armed Forces of the Philippines. It is responsible for defending the country's maritime borders, ensuring the safety of its seas, and supporting national defense efforts. The Navy operates various ships, including frigates, landing docks, and patrol vessels.

BRP Jose Rizal

A modern frigate of the Philippine Navy used for patrolling and combat missions.

BRP Davao del Sur

A landing dock vessel used for transporting troops, vehicles, and humanitarian missions.

BRP Gregorio del Pilar

A patrol ship used for monitoring Philippine waters and maritime security.
